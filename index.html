Our Mission

Our mission is to catalyze the rapid integration of cutting-edge scientific research into public policy and community awareness, with a particular focus on issues affecting public health and safety. We recognize that there's often a significant lag between new scientific discoveries and their implementation in regulations or public knowledge, potentially putting communities at risk. Our goal is to create a more responsive and efficient system for translating critical scientific findings into actionable policies and widespread public understanding.

We aim to identify emerging health and safety concerns where recent research suggests a need for policy updates or increased public awareness. The issue of manganese levels in baby formula and its potential impact on infant brain development serves as one crucial example of our work. However, our scope extends to a wide range of topics where accelerated information flow could significantly benefit public wellbeing, from environmental hazards to food safety and beyond.

Our Working Plan

  • Monitoring and summarizing recent scientific literature across various fields related to public health and safety.
  • Developing clear, accessible communications to explain complex scientific concepts to policymakers and the general public.
  • Building networks connecting researchers, policymakers, advocacy groups, and community leaders to facilitate faster information exchange.
  • Advocating for more agile policy-making processes that can quickly respond to new scientific evidence.
  • Empowering communities with knowledge to make informed decisions and advocate for their own health and safety.

Join Us

We invite concerned citizens, students, educators, scientists, and policymakers to join us in this critical endeavor. By working together to speed up the flow of vital information from labs to laws and from research papers to public awareness, we can create a more responsive, science-driven approach to protecting public health and safety.

Our Vision

Our vision is a society where emerging threats are swiftly identified and addressed, and where all communities benefit equally from the latest scientific understanding, ensuring a safer, healthier future for everyone.
